Popular Places to Hangout:

Babbio Patio
This is located outside the Front Gate of Babbio Center, wehre a large tent with several tables exist.

Gateway North & South
These are two connected buildings with a overbridge across the Sixth Street. There are plenty of rooms for classes in Gateway South and places for students to do selfstudy in Gateway North.

Tents outside of Humphreys Lawn
Several tents are set outside of Humphreys Hall. This would be a great place to visit if you stayed indoors for too long due to the covid pandemic.

Samuel C Williams Library
If you want to study somewhere, Samuel C Williams Library would be your best choice. This library is equipped with several private meeting rooms, dozens of tables in different sizes, and hundreds of seats prepared for your A+ in all your subjects.
A little whisper: go to the top floor if you really want to study, and make sure that you don't make any noise at all.

The Cannon
Stay frosty, this one cannot fire (cuz I've never seen it firing).But still, try not to put attention on firing it.
Do you want to know what it feels like to have a cannon targeting at Manhattan? This cannon fulfills your desire of destruction if you have the ability of imagination.
This cannon is located near Howe Building, where you enter the entrance by walking straight from the hallway outside the south gate.

The Lawn by Castle Point
One of the best places to have a chat or study outside the rooms. You can see Pier 13 from this spot and nobody comes here.
This lawn is located at the southwest side of the gate of Castle Point.

Babbio Center
Babbio Center is really beautiful if you enter from the Gate that towards the Hudson River. In advance, this building contains several seats mainly on 2nd and 3rd floor that you can just have a seat and kill time.
This building is located at the southeast side of the crossroads of Sixth Street and River Street.

Popular Food Places:

Pierce Dining Hall
A place where you can spend over 10 USD for a buffet. This place opens from 6 A.M. to 12 A.M. and 7 days in a week.
This dining hall is located at the 2nd floor of Howe Building.

Pierce Cafe
A place where you can get coffee, snacks, and some limited grill options after the Dining Hall closes. This place opens from 6 A.M. to 12 A.M. and 7 days in a week.
This dining hall is located at the 2nd floor of Howe Building, in front of the entrance to the Pierce Dining Hall

Colonel John's
